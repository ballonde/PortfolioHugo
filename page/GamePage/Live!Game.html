● Game Concept

We play a little robot who need to explore a abandoned complexe, but, there are dangerous robot lurking and each action consumme energy. Its an infiltration game where you need to be cureful of your movement in wich you are limited.

Explication

My work

In this project I was in charge of the 3C and the ennemies. For the 3C, make the movement of the character and is energy system, for the ennemies patrouille and pursuite system.

● Little robot

To be able to infiltrate the character as some movements abilities.It permitted to have acces to place ennemies don't have or to ousmart them.

These movements are linked to a energy system each time one of the movement abilities it use (exept the walk) energy is consumed.

Movement:

- Walk

- Run

- Jump

- Crouch

Energy system: constraint on use of movement in infiltration

The character have an energy bar. Each time an action is performed the energy decrease. When the energy is empty the character is deconnected and can't react anymore, it need to be reboot.

when the player is in reboot phase he need to make a point and click.After that, the energy max decrease and the energy bar is fill to the new amount. To completly restor the max energy, the player need to find some battery in the map.

● Ennemies

There only one ennemis in the game. His comportement is simple, the ennemis has a patrol comportement and if a player is detected during it, it switch to a pursuit comportement. During the puirsuit he run after the player. If he lose the player he come back to his patrol, otherwise he kill the player.

There where an issue with the vision node, so I didn't know how to detect if a player leaves a vision of an ennemies.

Patrol

They are several type of patrol for enemies.

-Immobile: just see what in front of him.

-Rotate: don't move but rototate regularly.

-Patrouille: follow a series of objectif point in the world.

-Patrouille with rotatation: follow a series of objectif point in the world and chek the souronding at each location.

Pursuit of an player

Ennemies have a vision sens, if a player is seen they will stop what they doing and start run after him.

whene they run after a player they lunch continous raycast. If the raycast can touch the target it mean the target is visible. If it can't, it means the target is too far away or there is an obstacle. In both case the target is considered escaped. There a small delay before an ennemies who lost his target come back.
