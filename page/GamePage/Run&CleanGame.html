● Game Concept

It's a 2D runners where the player need to dodge the incoming obstacle and collect the ressource. With theses ressources he fight the bosses.

Explication

My work

This project was a mobile game in wich I worked on 3C and boss fight. I adjusted the camera, position of player character and the physics for the movement in the games. The boss need to be able to have simple pattern attack by shooting projectile, and difficulty level.

Mouvement: 2d movement

Generally in runners the position and angle of the camera is very important, you need to see the obstacle coming. That why the character is on the left side with most of the visibility on the obstacle coming from the right.

There is a double jump. we have done many experimentation to adjuste the speed of jumping and falling, This need to not be a issue to dodge obstacle.

UI: restriction of the mobile support

The aren't a lot of UI, two button and one symbol for indicate how many projectile the player have.

But the support is a lot different because of his size.

● Boss pattern

Boss are in two phases, in the first phase a boss appear and attack the player. He disappered shortly after. This happened several time during the level, the exact number depend of the difficulty. The objectif of this phase is to prepare player to fight the boss by showing some pattern and if is skillful enough, to wound him in advance.

The second phase is the final fight, the boss don't desappeared anymore. An attack from the boss as two part: choose a projectile to shot and a zone to attack. They are two types of projectile, the simple will stun the player upon contact making him lose time. The advanced will make him lose some of his projectile. The running area are divide in three parts depending of the height. The chosen projectile will be shoot in one or two parts. The probabilities of the pattern will depend of the difficulties.
